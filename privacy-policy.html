Privacy Policy for DroidOS

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DroidOS. This Privacy Policy explains how KatsuyaMaki LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our DroidOS mobile application (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Audio Information: If you grant permission, we may collect audio from your device's microphone. This is used exclusively for the voice input and dictation features within the DroidOS Keyboard & Trackpad module. The audio is processed in real-time for speech-to-text conversion and is not stored or shared.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the Service, such as the features you use and the actions you take. This helps us understand how to improve the app. This data is anonymized and does not personally identify you.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device, such as the operating system version, to ensure compatibility and provide support.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve the DroidOS experience. This includes:

  • Core App Functionality: Permissions such as "Draw over other apps" (`SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW`) are required to display the floating launcher and keyboard/trackpad on your screen. The "Run foreground service" permission is used to keep these components active. The "Control vibration" permission provides haptic feedback.
  • Voice Input: The "Record audio" permission is used solely to capture audio for the speech-to-text dictation feature.
  • App Improvement: Anonymized usage data helps us identify which features are most popular and where we can make improvements.
  • Notifications: The "Post notifications" permission is used to display a persistent notification for the foreground service, which is required by Android.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent;
  • To comply with a legal obligation;
  • To protect and defend the rights or property of KatsuyaMaki LLC;
  • In connection with a merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
