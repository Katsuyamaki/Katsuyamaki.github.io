Free - always

Full-featured tiling window manager with keyboard/trackpad companion app.

  • Queue-based tiling window manager
  • Floating launcher bubble with app drawer
  • Keyboard and trackpad companion app
  • External automation support (Tasker, MacroDroid, ADB)
  • Workspace profiles and custom layouts (unlimited)

New in v5.0 - free for all Community users

  • Smart Auto Margin + IME sync - launcher margins, keyboard, and dock stay in sync for seamless typing across tiled windows
  • Full-screen + tiled app coexistence - use tiled apps alongside full-screen Android apps with automatic transitions and keyboard compatibility
  • Expanded real minimize - truly hide apps off-screen on Samsung DeX (A10-16) and virtual mirror mode (A14 and below), not just corner-hide
  • Smart app window tracking - correctly detect and tile apps that open multiple windows or sub-screens (for example, Google Docs opening a separate document), with enhanced Samsung DeX compatibility
  • Orientation-aware layouts and profiles - keep separate layout/profile behavior for portrait and landscape automatically
  • New broadcast commands - Open & Move To, Open & Swap, Manual Retile, and more for advanced automation workflows
  • Manual re-tile command - double tap the floating app bubble to manually reapply the current app queue and layouts
  • Improved stability, Android 15 compatibility, and dozens of bug fixes

FAQ

Is the Community version really free?
Yes. DroidOS Community is free and always will be. v5.0 adds major free improvements including Smart Auto Margin, full-screen app coexistence, expanded real minimize, smart app window tracking, and orientation-aware layouts/profiles. Paid modules add additional v5.0+ power-user features like custom themes, advanced profiles, Dynamic Tiling, Peek Mode, and app lock mode.
How do I get my license key after purchase?
Use the Retrieve License Key page with your Paddle purchase email and the transaction ID from your Paddle receipt email (for example, txn_...).
What's the difference between buying modules and Pro?
Individual modules unlock specific feature sets. Pro unlocks both current modules plus every future module we build. If you plan to use DroidOS long-term, Pro is the better value.
How long is Early Access pricing available?
The 15% launch discount is available through April 10, 2026, or until the first 200 paid activations, whichever comes first.
What does Early Access mean?
Core workflows are stable and ready to use. We are actively improving edge-case compatibility and shipping frequent updates based on user feedback.
How many devices can I use?
Each license (module or Pro) can be activated on up to 3 devices. You can deactivate a device at any time to free up a slot.
What happens if I cancel Pro Annual?
Your Pro features remain active until the end of your billing period. After that, you keep access to the free Community features.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. We offer a 14-day refund policy on all purchases. See our Terms of Service for details.
Is the DroidOS Keyboard/Trackpad app free?
Yes. The keyboard/trackpad companion app is free across all tiers. Paid keyboard modules may be offered in the future.
